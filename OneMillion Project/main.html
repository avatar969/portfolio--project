About Us

1MILLION Dance Studio began in a basement studio in 2014. Since then, it has continued to grow and welcome students of all ages, backgrounds and experience levels. Located in Seoul, South Korea, 1MILLION’s facility holds two fully equipped studios and a full-time staff who speaks both English and Korean. 1MILLION invites you to explore your creative potential and find a community among mentors and peers that never cease to INSPIRE MILLIONS.
